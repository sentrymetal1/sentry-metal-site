About Sentry Metal

Custom fabrication since 1970.

A family-run shop in Lockport, NY with deep experience in carbon steel, stainless, and aluminum work for industrial clients.

Built on quality, integrity, and experience.

Sentry Metal Services was founded in 1970 with a simple proposition: fabricate the job right, on time, and stand behind it. More than fifty years later, we still operate that way.

Our shop floor brings together over a century of combined fabrication experience. That depth shows up in the small things — clean welds, square assemblies, drawings that match what gets delivered — and in the big things: complex multi-ton structural builds that have to fit perfectly the first time.

We work for industrial clients who need a partner, not a vendor. The projects we're proudest of are the ones we've been quoting for the same customers for fifteen, twenty, thirty years.

What We Stand For

  • Quality first Inspections at every stage. We don't ship work we wouldn't accept on our own job.
  • Integrity in pricing Honest quotes, scope-tight estimates, no surprises mid-project.
  • Experience on the floor Our welders and fitters have been doing this for decades. It shows.
  • Schedule discipline We commit to dates we can hit. When something changes, you hear it from us first.
  • Long-term partnership Most of our work is repeat business. That's the standard we measure ourselves against.
